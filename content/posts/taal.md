<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tala_(music)#Common_Hindustani_talas">taals in Hindustani music</a>.
Here I'll list some songs I know from each taal.

## Teentaal (तीनताल)

* Structure: 16 = 4 + 4 + 4 + 4.
* Listen on tabla: [140 bpm](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SxRMsYre02k).

Most songs are in teentaal.

[fast (250 bpm)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d4wA-gD1WKs).
